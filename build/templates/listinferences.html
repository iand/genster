{{if .Summary}}{{.Summary}}{{else}}“Inferences” refer to hints and suggestions that help fill in missing information in the family tree.{{end}}

There are various types of inferences that can appear here, such as:

year of birth
the most recent year of birth can be estimated based on known events like baptisms, marriages, or the birth of the first child.
year of death
the year of death can be estimated based on the date of burial, if available, or set to be after the last firmly known event date.
general facts
some tags are applied based on cause of death or text in other events.
